## 🚀 **QUICK START: What do I do first?**

1.  **Check the Market Ticker (Top Bar):**
    *   Look at the valid **Top Black Bar**.
    *   **Brent Crude / WTI:** Is oil rising? Tell customers prices might go up.
    *   **USD/INR:** Is Rupee weak? Import costs will rise. Use this to create urgency.

2.  **Go to "Sales Workspace" (Tab 2):**
    *   This is your "Deal Room".
    *   Select **Client** & **Source**.
    *   See the **Map**, **Landed Cost**, and **Talking Points** instantly.

3.  **Check "CRM & Tasks" (Tab 12):**
    *   See who you need to call today.
    *   Don't guess—just follow the list.

## 📚 **DETAILED FEATURE GUIDE**

### 1. 💼 Sales Workspace ("The Deal Room")
**Goal:** Create a perfect quote in 30 seconds.
*   **Source:** Select where to lift material (Refinery/Port).
*   **Destination:** Select Customer City.
*   **Map Intelligence:** Confirms route distance instantly.
*   **Landed Cost Engine:** Shows Base Price + Freight + 18% GST.
*   **Sales Scripts:**
    *   *Objection Handling:* If client says "Too High", click the dropdown for the perfect reply.
    *   *Confidence Meter:* Tells you if delivery is "Low Risk" or "High Risk".
*   **Output:** Click **"Generate PDF Quote"** to get a professional offer letter instantly.

### 2. 🎯 CRM & Tasks (Client Relationship)
**Goal:** Never miss a follow-up.
*   **Today's Worklist:** Your daily "To-Do" list.
*   **Auto-Tasks:**
    *   Did you send a quote? System reminds you to call in 2 hours.
    *   New Enquiry? System tells you to call in 15 mins.
    *   Payment Pending? System sets a daily reminder.
*   **Actions:** Click "✅ Done" when you finish a call.

### 3. 🚨 SOS Sales Blast (Special Price)
**Goal:** Sell fast when prices drop.
*   **How it works:** If a refinery drops rates by ₹200+, this tab lights up.
*   **Target List:** System automatically finds customers in that city who buy that grade.
*   **WhatsApp Blast:** Click the button to copy a "Price Drop Alert" message to send immediately.

### 4. 👥 Ecosystem Management (Data Entry)
**Goal:** Manage your Contact Database.
*   **Sales & Clients:** Add your Customers here.
    *   *Category:* Select "Big Trader", "Contractor", etc. for better reports.
*   **Source & Supply:** Add new Refineries or Importers.
*   **Import/Export:** Upload your Excel sheet of contacts here.

### 5. 📅 Sales Calendar
**Goal:** Know the best time to sell.
*   **Red Dates:** Holidays/No-Entry days. Avoid delivery commitments.
*   **Green Dates:** High demand days. Push for sales.

### 6. 🏭 Feasibility & Logistics
**Goal:** Check if a delivery is profitable.
*   **Route Check:** See specific road restrictions or bridge issues (simulated).
*   **Freight Choice:** Compare "Tanker vs Drum" costs.

## ❓ **FAQ: "What do I enter here?"**

*   **Q: What if I don't know the exact distance?**
    *   A: Don't worry. The system now has a "Safety Net". If you enter a new city, it estimates a safe freight cost and warns you in **RED**.

*   **Q: How do I send a quote on WhatsApp?**
    *   A: In the "Sales Workspace", click the **"WhatsApp"** button. It formats a professional message automatically.

*   **Q: Why is the Margin Slider there?**
    *   A: You control the profit. Slide it to adjust.
    *   *Warning:* If you go below ₹400/MT, the system alerts you "Low Margin".

*   **Q: Where do I see global oil prices?**
    *   A: Always at the **Top of the Screen**. It updates live (if internet is connected).
