Frequently Asked Questions

How is the 25% discount calculated?
Each feature is benchmarked against equivalent market tools (Salesforce, Bloomberg, FourKites, etc.). The market price is the fair mid-range rate for similar functionality. PPS prices are set at 75% of these market rates — a flat 25% discount across every feature.
Can I upgrade between tiers?
Yes. You can upgrade at any time. When upgrading mid-cycle, you only pay the prorated difference for the remaining billing period. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
Is this per-user or per-company pricing?
All listed prices are per-user, per-month (billed annually). Volume discounts are available for teams of 5+ users. Contact us for enterprise pricing with unlimited seats.
What is included in Priority Support (Ultimate)?
Ultimate subscribers get a 4-hour response SLA, a dedicated account manager, custom AI model training for your specific market segment, and white-label options for client-facing reports.
Are there any setup or onboarding fees?
No. All plans include free onboarding, data migration assistance, and a 2-week guided implementation period. Training sessions are included at no extra cost.
What about data security and compliance?
PPS Dashboard runs on your own infrastructure. All data stays within your network. The platform supports role-based access control, audit logging, and is built for Indian regulatory compliance including GST.
