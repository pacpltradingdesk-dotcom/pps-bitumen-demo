# 📖 PPS Anantam Dashboard — User Guide
### Master Feature Inventory (1-line purpose per page)

> **Kis ke liye?** Prince P Shah sir ki bitumen trading business ke liye. 4 roles — Director, Sales, Operations, Viewer.
> **Kaise navigate karein?** Top bar mein 7 modules + "More ▾" mein 2 aur. Har module ke andar sidebar mein pages.
> **Login:** Default `admin / 0000` — 24hr session (tab close karke bhi wapas aa sakte ho).

---

## 🏠 MODULE 1: HOME
**Kya hai?** Roz subah sabse pehle yahan aao. Market status + KPIs + alerts ek jagah.

| Page | Kya karta hai | Kab use karo |
|------|---------------|--------------|
| **Command Center** | Executive dashboard — 5 KPIs (Brent, WTI, USD/INR, VG30, AI Signal), alerts, quick actions | Roz subah 5 min ke liye |
| **Live Market** | Real-time crude + USD/INR + VG30 prices, BUY/SELL indicators | Jab market move ho raha ho |
| **Opportunities** | AI ne khud detect kiye naye deals — WhatsApp template ready | Din mein 2-3 baar check karo |
| **Director Cockpit** | Prince sir ke liye: yesterday summary + today priorities + 15-day outlook | Director login pe |
| **Client Showcase** | Public-facing demo page (marketing) | Client ko link bhejne ke liye |
| **Subscription Pricing** | SaaS pricing tiers display | Business development |

## 🎯 "DAY IN THE LIFE" — Role-based Journeys

### 👔 Director (Prince sir) — Morning 15 min
1. **Home > Command Center** — KPI + alerts scan
2. **Home > Director Cockpit** — yesterday summary + 15-day outlook
3. **Intelligence > Market Signals** — BULLISH/BEARISH composite
4. **Reports > Director Brief** — download 6-page PDF

### 💼 Sales Person — Morning 30 min
1. **Sales > CRM Tasks** — today's worklist
2. **Sales > Daily Log** — log yesterday's meetings
3. **Intelligence > News** — kya update hai
4. Customer call → **Pricing > Pricing Calculator** → **Sales > Comm Hub** → send quote
5. Complex negotiation → **Sales > Negotiation AI** — brief pack

### 🚚 Operations — Order se Dispatch Flow
1. Order aaya → **Pricing > Pricing Calculator** — finalize pricing
2. **Documents > Document Mgmt** — create PO/SO
3. **Logistics > Feasibility** — best route decide
4. **Logistics > Tanker Tracking** — track dispatch
5. **Compliance > E-Way Bill** — generate e-way bill

### 👀 Customer/Viewer (demo)
1. **Home > Client Showcase** — marketing page
2. **Home > Live Market** — current prices
3. **Home > Subscription Pricing** — plans
